How It Works

About the Model

📊

Dataset

Trained on the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset — a benchmark dataset with 768 samples from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases.

🤖

Algorithm

Logistic Regression with StandardScaler normalization. The model predicts the binary probability of Type 2 Diabetes onset.

🧬

Features

8 clinical features: Pregnancies, Glucose, Blood Pressure, Skin Thickness, Insulin, BMI, Diabetes Pedigree Function, and Age.

🎯

Performance

Achieves approximately 78% accuracy on held-out test data with an 80/20 stratified train-test split.
